Comparison: Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity is a term used to describe the amount of water vapour in air. Latitude is an angle which ranges from 0° at the equator to 90° either North or South at the poles.

Warm air can hold more water than cool air. The lower the latitude value, the higher likelihood of humidity given its proximity to the equator. Given this information, I would agree that Latitude does in fact have an impact on humidity.

The ideal relative humidity for health and comfort is about 40% - 50%. During the winter months, it should be lower than 40% to avoid condensation on the windows.
